Note from Elise

I am a firm believer in keeping a strong, open line of communication between home and school. After all, we have something so special in common; your child ! As partners in your child's education we all work toward the same goal, we want your child to succeed in school and in life.
Part of my job is to ensure that information flows steadily and effectively between yourselves and the school. I want you to be sure that if you have any concerns or suggestions related to the school or your child wellbeing you can contact me.
As your Parent Coordinator, my goals are to motivate, empower and assist you by creating wonderful programs and events that adhere to the curriculum in addition to being innovative and memorable.

L'Etoile Parent Committee

The goal of the parent committee is to find fun and meaningful ways for parents to participate in their child's learning at L'Etoile and to strengthen community. Parent involvement takes many forms including: monthly parent coffees, potlucks and picnics, informational seminars, movie nights and more!
Please use this link to sign up for the various sub-committees. Elise will let families know about upcoming events and forums but feel free to contact her anytime.
